1. Introduction of Slovakia

Slovakia is a nation in Central Europe that is landlocked. Hungary to the south, Austria to the west, Poland to the north, Ukraine to the east, and the Czech Republic to the northwest are its borders.Slovakia has a population of more than 5.4 million people living within its 49,000 square kilometres of primarily mountainous area. Bratislava is the capital and largest city; Koice is the second largest.In Slovakia, a small number of cities and towns make up the slightly over half of the population that lives in or near large urban centers. With 583,600 residents, Bratislava is both the capital and largest city. Bratislava is situated on the borders of Hungary and Austria on the Danube River. Bratislava is not only Slovakias capital but also its hub for tourism and culture.

The necessary data set was obtained from the coronavirus package in R.This includes information about top 201 countries that have been affected from Covid-19. Data includes details from 2020-01-22 to 2023-03-09. The description of data is as follows :

  • date - Date of data collectoin
  • province - Specific province of the country
  • country - Name of the country
  • lat - Latitude of the country
  • long - Longitude of the country
  • type - Type of the case reported(confirmed, recovered, death)
  • cases - Number of cases reported
